Water Resources and Irrigation

Sector >> Water Resources and Irrigation

Water resource sector is under severe stress to meet the demands of the growing population and the competing requirements from the economic sectors. Decreasing availability of fresh water, depleting ground water resources, land degradation, shortage of water for irrigation and increasing climate variability leading to unpredictable rainfall and shifting seasons would continue to pose serious challenges for water resource management and to thwart potential water conflicts at different levels.

Our work in this sector has been focused on assisting clients and partners in design and preparation of DPR for irrigation schemes, institutional strengthening and project management consultancy, technical assistance for construction and supervision, monitoring and evaluation, environment impact assessments, training and capacity development, Participatory Irrigation Management and Irrigation Management Transfers to Water Users Associations.

Select Work in Water Resources and Irrigation

Minor Lift Irrigation Planning and Implementation for Orissa Integrated Irrigated Agriculture and water Management Investment Programe (OIIAWMIP)

2009 - 2011

Consulting services for Irrigation Activities under JOHAR (Jharkhand Opportunities for Harnessing Rural Growth) project Zone-I

2017 - 2020

End Term/Impact Evaluation Study of Micro Irrigation Scheme under Per Drop More Crop component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

2018

The Odisha Integrated Irrigated Agriculture and Water Management Investment Programme (OIIAWMIP) was conceptualized with the support of the Asian Development Bank (ADB) to maximize the state irrigation potential and for the continued development of water resources sector. The project had a specific component of Minor Lift Irrigation Rehabilitation wherein 1400 Lift Points were to be repaired and associated agricultural training and improvement take place.

Sutra Consulting with its consortium partner was engaged during 2009-11 in the first phase of the program as an implementation consultant for this lift irrigation component. The assignment involved feasibility study, design and DPR preparation, WUA strengthening, technical support for procurement of material and community led construction, supervision of work, water management training to farmers and post installation support. A large team of experts were deployed to support the implementation, promote agriculture intensification and institution building involving promotion of PIM and strengthening of Water Users Associations. The assignment included a strong Monitoring and Evaluation component wherein a monitoring plan was prepared and installed in the Lift irrigation Cell to review project progress and processes. The lift irrigation component was one of the successful implementation cases during first phase of the project.

Sutra Consulting has been appointed for implementation of irrigation for Jharkhand Opportunities for harnessing Rural Growth (JOHAR) project funded by World Bank for the duration of 3 years from June 2017 to August 2020. This project is being implemented by the Jharkhand State Livelihoods Promotion Society (JSLPS) under the aegis of the Department of Rural Development, Government of Jharkhand (GoJ) with the objective of enhancing and diversifying the household income in selected farm and non-farm sectors for targeted beneficiaries in the project areas.

As the project implementation agency, Sutra is responsible to provide services for design of small irrigation systems, supervision during implementation of these systems by community institutions, development of training manual for Para irrigation engineer and water users group, training to a cadre of Para-irrigation engineers for designing similar systems and training to a cadre of operators for these systems. It also has to assess and finalise the potential water sources in the target areas, assess static head through field survey, detailed design based on field observations, through participatory approach and prepare detailed project reports of each irrigation scheme, each process of micro irrigation scheme has to be community procurement and operation, sutra's role is limited to supervise the implementation of irrigation schemes etc.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) - a centrally sponsored scheme of Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry and Cooperative, Ministry of Agriculture and Government of Jharkhand. The programme is aimed at establishing micro irrigation schemes and Increase the area under micro irrigation technologies to enhance water use efficiency. The programme would contribute in improving productivity of crops and income of farmers through precision water management, adoption of micro irrigation technologies in water intensive/consuming crops like sugarcane, banana, cotton etc. and give adequate focus to extend coverage of field crops under micro irrigation technologies, promote potential use of micro irrigation systems for promoting fertigation and establish convergence and synergy with activities of on-going programmes and create employment opportunities.

Sutra consulting, provided evaluation support the programme and the team of experts evaluated the Planning Process in the District to provide feedback on the adequacy and relevance of District Irrigation Plan (DIP), recommended benchmarks leading to functioning of District Level Committees and insights of fun flow and effective programme management. The evaluation process has contributed in giving insights on proper targeting process and beneficiary selection, physical and financial progress, adequate processes to be followed in system installation and constraints among farmers in using fertigation through MI system.

MELD under IWMP in the four districts (Aurangabad, Kaimur, Munger and Rohtas) of Bihar

2016 - 2021

Baseline and Environment and Social Management Framework for Andhra Pradesh Integrated Irrigation Agriculture Transformation Project (APIIATP)

2017-2018

Sutra Consulting is engaged as the Monitoring, Evaluation, Learning and Documentation Agency for the period of December 2016 to December 2021 for the Integrated Watershed management programme in four districts of Bihar supported by the Bihar watershed Development Society. The assignment entailed monitoring and documenting the Natural Resources and community ownership and engagement based outcomes in approximately 322 Micro Watersheds/Sub-Watersheds across 4 districts of Bihar. The work of Sutra as MELD agency includes conducting a baseline study, devising indicators for process monitoring, preparing frameworks for impact evaluation, and devising framework for output-input monitoring, documenting best practises and maintaining a regular line of communication and reporting.

The Government of Andhra Pradesh has proposed a next generation irrigated agriculture transformative project with climate resilience as its cross-cutting theme. The Project Development Objective is to enhance agricultural productivity, profitability and resilience to climate variability in selected tank systems of Andhra Pradesh. The project beneficiaries will include small and marginal farmers, water users associations, farmer producer organizations, and other agro-entrepreneurs. During project preparation, specific target areas will be identified and specific interventions will be designed to benefit women and other vulnerable groups. The project activities would be grouped into four main components: Improving Irrigated Agriculture Efficiency at Farm Level; Adaptive Sustainable Agriculture Practices; Climate-friendly Market and Agribusiness Promotion; and Project Management and Capacity Building.

Sutra Consulting provided two sets of technical services to the programme. Our team of experts conducted a baseline assessment to study the pre-project socio-economic and institutional status of existing agricultural and fisheries-based livelihood systems in selected smallholder irrigation systems (tank systems) covering a set of project and control tanks. Our advisors also undertook identification and assessment environmental and social management measures in respect of the improving tank systems performance and resilience, improving irrigation efficiency, inflow hydrology management, improving productivity, adapting sustainable agriculture practices, innovative and technology transfer for fisheries production and agri business promotion. A number of critical programme design documents including the Tribal People Planning Framework, Gender Action Plan, Integrated Nutrition Management Plan and Environmental Management Plans were developed by our team of advisors and have been widely disseminated.
